Content Library and Curriculum on KidsLoop 2.0

Introducing the KidsLoop Hummingbird Curriculum

KidsLoop Hummingbird Curriculum is the ESL familiarization program for preschoolers in Vietnam compiled according to the curriculum framework specified in Circular 50 of the Ministry of Education and Training. The course materials are high quality Badanamu resources from KidsLoop Global. The KidsLoop Hummingbird Curriculum Council consists of Academic Specialists from KidsLoop Vietnam, Lecturers of English Department - Ho Chi Minh City University of Education with the consultation of KidsLoop Global Academic Advisory Board. The KidsLoop Hummingbird curriculum has three levels suitable for Kindergarten (Mầm - Chồi - Lá). KidsLoop Hummingbird program distribution offers a total of 35 weeks per level with two English introductory activities a week. Each activity lasts 30 minutes.

New Global Partnershipsi

We have signed NDAs, Agreements with our new partners: Oxford University Press Shanghai, Nathan, Diyi, Zaprendo, and Shrewburrys School. Oxford University Press (OUP) Shanghai is a department of the University of Oxford dedicated to the publication of educational, reference, academic, and general materials to contribute to educational progress. Nathan is a French educational company that has been designing and producing educational games and resources for over a century. It is one of the leading brands for European early childhood education. Diyi is a joint venture founded by Shanghai Education Publishing House and NamiBox. Zaprendo, a UK-based global EdTech developer dedicated to language acquisition and literacy, contributes their educational content to the Green School, an international global education network. Shrewsbury School is a large independent school in the UK with international branches in Hong Kong and Bangkok.

KidsLoop Việt Nam

Training course about KidsLoop Hummingbird and preparing KidsLoop Sample Classes for Teachers from three Practicing Schools in Hanoi (April 19-20, 2021)

On April 19-20, KidsLoop Vietnam organized a training course about teaching KidsLoop Hummingbird curriculum and preparing for KidsLoop Sample Classes for teachers of three practising schools in (Hanoi). There were 26 teachers attending the training course. The training course took place with an exciting, focused and effective atmosphere. After two training days, the teachers were able to use the KidsLoop 2.0 platform to explore the content library, digitize the traditional materials into interactive and engaging digital learning materials for children.

After the training course, KidsLoop was officially deployed in three practice preschools from May 2021. From the positive signals from three practice preschools, KidsLoop VN is also preparing a product deployment plan to public schools in the North from May 2021.

KidsLoop VN started implementing in the South and Thu Duc City - Ho Chi Minh City
On April 26, 2021, KidsLoop Vietnam signed a distribution contract with our distribution partner in the East-west and South-West regions and Ho Chi Minh City. Our strategic distributor will implement their business with KidsLoop platform and content in turn in Thu Duc city, Con Dao district (BR-VT), Phan Thiet city (Binh Thuan), some towns and districts in Ca Mau province and Ben Tre province. KidsLoop sample classes in Thu Duc city are expected to start from mid-May 2021.

TOT (training of trainers) course for the distribution partner in Ho Chi Minh City and the East-West and the South West region.
On May 7-8, 2021, at the Ho Chi Minh City University of Education, KidsLoop Vietnam organized a TOT course for 20 trainees who are KidsLoop's distribution partners’ education managers, teachers and staff. The training course content includes information about KidsLoop and instruction on using the KidsLoop platform for in-class, live, and blended teaching learning. After the course, trainees were granted certificates of completion for the ToT from KidsLoop Global.

Workshop to introduce KidsLoop platform and KidsLoop Hummingbird curriculum to parents.
On 9/5/2021, in Thu Duc City, and 15-16/5 on Phan Thiet city (Binh Thuan province) KidsLoop Vietnam cooperated with our distribution partner to organize a workshop to introduce KidsLoop platform and ESL familiarization program - KidsLoop Hummingbird. At the workshop, parents and children were introduced to KidsLoop, and participated in the demo classroom of KidsLoop Hummingbird. From June 2021, parents and students in the Thu Duc city and Phan Thiet city area will start participating in KidsLoop sample classes. The workshop is the first in the series of workshops to introduce KidsLoop to Thu Duc city, Phan Thiet city, Con Dao in May 2021. KidsLoop and KidsLoop Hummingbird sample classes will be started in these regions from Jun 2021.
